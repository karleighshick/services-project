Our Healthcare Services

At Eloheh, we believe in offering care that nurtures the body, mind, and spirit. Explore our wide range of services designed to bring balance and health to your life.

1. Primary & Preventative Care Services

  • Annual health check-ups & screenings
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, heart health)
  • Vaccination & immunization clinics
  • Preventative lab testing (cholesterol, blood sugar, hormone balance)
  • Women’s health services (wellness exams, prenatal support)
  • Men’s health services (prostate screenings, vitality programs)
  • Pediatric wellness check-ups
    • Learn more about Preventive Care

2. Mental & Emotional Health Services

  • Individual and family counseling
  • Group therapy sessions (stress, grief, addiction recovery support)
  • Tele-therapy and virtual mental health coaching
  • Stress management workshops
  • Mindfulness & meditation programs
  • Substance abuse recovery programs

3. Holistic & Integrative Health Services

  • Nutritional counseling and meal planning
  • Herbal medicine consultations
  • Acupuncture & traditional healing therapies
  • Yoga and movement therapy classes
  • Sleep health consultations
  • Preventative lifestyle coaching

4. Community & Outreach Programs

  • Mobile health clinics (for rural or underserved areas)
  • Health fairs and free wellness screenings
  • School-based health education (nutrition, exercise, mental health awareness)
  • Workplace wellness programs (stress reduction, ergonomics, resilience training)
  • Chronic illness support groups
  • Indigenous health and wellness knowledge-sharing sessions

5. Specialized Services

  • Physical therapy & rehabilitation
  • Pain management clinics (non-opioid, holistic focus)
  • Maternal & child health programs
  • Geriatric care services (aging gracefully, fall prevention, memory support)
  • Hospice and palliative care with holistic emphasis
  • Integrative oncology support (nutrition, mindfulness, alternative therapies alongside treatment)
  • Telehealth urgent care

6. Wellness & Lifestyle Services

  • Fitness and movement classes (personalized programs)
  • Smoking cessation programs
  • Weight management and metabolic health services
  • Digital health app support (tracking habits, coaching through an Eloheh app)
  • Retreats focused on rest, renewal, and holistic health
